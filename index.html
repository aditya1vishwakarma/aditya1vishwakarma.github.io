Aditya Vishwakarma

Writer/Director based in the Bay Area


Aditya Vishwakarma is a 22-year-old science-fiction and drama writer/director living in the San Francisco Bay Area. Recently, he has developed feature films such as “Inside Jokes” and “Relentless” and is developing a dystopian cyberpunk feature titled “Collision Course”. When he’s not writing about exciting possibilities the future of the planet holds, he is photographing the world he appreciates so much. With his rich life experiences and Silicon Valley roots. he loves to write about characters finding themselves in the midst of life-changing journeys.

A Few Of My Works

Colission Course

A brainwashed human soldier finds himself back on Earth in the year 2172 to a planet where free will doesn't seem to exist anymore. He must save the Earthlings from the evil corporations that created this dystopia.

Inside Jokes

A standup comedian who stumbles upon the opportunity of a lifetime realizes that it comes with a big ask. He must decide if he values his dream enough to take the necessary steps to get where he wants to be.

Relentless

A promising basketball player will do whatever it take to prove everyone that he's not a nobody after he is relocated to a small college team. His biggest hurdle, his ego gets in the way when he realizes he cannot do it all alone.

Crossroads

Short: An athlete recovering from injury loses all hope after recievinig bad news, and now must decide if his dreams are truly worth it, even if no one believes in him. He finds guidance from an unexpected source.
